About Us

We are an IoT-focused company building sustainable waste management solutions for smarter cities. Our mission is to empower communities with smart technology to manage waste effectively, sustainably, and affordably.

How It Works

  • IoT Sensors: Installed in bins to detect fill levels, temperature, and waste type.
  • Cloud Connectivity: Data is transmitted in real-time for centralized monitoring.
  • AI Optimization: Algorithms analyze trends to schedule timely collections.
  • Smart Routing: Dynamic routing cuts down fuel use and boosts efficiency.
  • Live Dashboard: City managers can monitor, alert, and plan using real-time insights.

Eco-Friendly

Reduces carbon emissions by optimizing collection routes and schedules based on actual fill levels.

Smart Disposal

Intelligent sensors detect fill levels and waste types to ensure proper sorting and disposal.

Real-time Monitoring

Track waste collection metrics and bin status with our comprehensive dashboard system.
